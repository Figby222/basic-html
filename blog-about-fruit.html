What is Fruit?

Now if you're novice out there, you may not know what fruit even is! Don't be afraid however, as with every lesson, you will be closer and closer to becoming the next fruit genius!

Fruit is a delacacy enjoyed by many around the world. A few great, yet complicated, examples of fruit include bananas, apples (one a day can keep the doctor away), and strawberries, but we will start with just mangoes as an introduction.

Mangoes - the Beginner Fruit

Mangoes are a juicy, delicious starter fruit that grow in tropical environments such as parts of Florida, California, and Hawaii. They grow on special, complicated-to-understand trees called mango trees. Complicated right? Don't worry, you'll soon have the basic knowledge to understand how farmers pick the mango from the tree (don't be alarmed by this complicated information).
